My Projects

I have had the pleasure to work on multiple personal and school projects, some of them include:

Local Experience Marketplace

The LME is a platform that connects tourists to local guides who can show them non-conventional spots in Rwanda, allowing them to experience the country through the eye of a local.

Study Buddy Finder

The Study Buddy Finder is a web application whose purpose is to provide a platform where students can interact with each and form study groups according to their availability and subject selection.
